Caramel Popcorn is a level 2 mutation obtained by fusing two [[Kernel-pult]]s.

It attacks the zombie with the highest max total hp in the lawn with a lobbed attack, but periodically lobs a blob of caramel which creates a slowing puddle.

If there are more than one [[Kernel-pult.canvas]]-types, the second instead attacks the second healthiest zombie, and so on for the next.
