Sap-fling is a level 3 mutation obtained by fusing a [[Kernel-pult]] and a [[Caramel Popcorn]], or by applying [[Fertilizer]] to a Caramel Popcorn.

It attacks the zombie with the highest max total hp in the lawn with a lobbed attack, which creates a slowing puddle.

If there are more than one [[Kernel-pult.canvas]]-types, the second instead attacks the second healthiest zombie, and so on for the next.
