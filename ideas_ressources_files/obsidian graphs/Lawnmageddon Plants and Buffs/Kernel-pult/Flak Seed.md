Flak Seed is a level 2 mutation obtained by fusing two [[Kernel-pult]]s, or by applying [[Fertilizer]] to a Kernel-pult.

It attacks the zombie with the highest max total hp in the lawn with a lobbed attack, firing three consecutive seeds.

If there are more than one [[Kernel-pult.canvas]]-types, the second instead attacks the second healthiest zombie, and so on for the next.
