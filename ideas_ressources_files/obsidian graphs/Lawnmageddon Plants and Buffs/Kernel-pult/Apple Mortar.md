Apple Mortar is a level 3 mutation obtained by splicing a [[Cabbage-pult]] and a [[Flak Seed]]

It attacks the zombie with the highest max total hp in the lawn with a lobbed attack, alongside two additional apples fired at the two closest zombies to the target. Each apple stalls the zombie.

If there are more than one [[Kernel-pult.canvas]]-types, the second instead attacks the second healthiest zombie, and so on for the next.
