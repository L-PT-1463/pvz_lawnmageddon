A.K.E.E. is a level 2 mutation obtained by fusing two [[Cabbage-pult]]s.

It attacks the zombie with the highest max total hp in the lawn with a lobbed attack, lobbing a seed that bounces to nearby zombies twice before disappearing.

If there are more than one [[Cabbage-pult.canvas]]-types, the second instead attacks the second healthiest zombie, and so on for the next.
