Pomegarnet Merchant is a level 3 mutation obtained by splicing fusing a [[Cabbage-pult]] and an [[A.K.E.E.]]

It attacks the zombie with the highest max total hp in the lawn with a lobbed attack, lobbing a garnet that bounces to nearby zombies. It has a larger bounce area than A.K.E.E. but can only bounce up to two times.

If there are more than one [[Cabbage-pult.canvas]]-types, the second instead attacks the second healthiest zombie, and so on for the next.
