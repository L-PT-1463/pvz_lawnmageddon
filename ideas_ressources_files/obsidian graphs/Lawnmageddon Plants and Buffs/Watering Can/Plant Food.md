Plant Food is a level 2 ability obtained by splicing a [[Watering Can]] and a [[Fertilizer]].

It activates a random potted plant's Plant Food power, which has a limited duration.

If a plant is already under the effects of a [[Watering Can.canvas]]-type ability's buff, this targets a different plant.
