Solar Tomato is a level 2 mutation obtained by splicing a [[Cherry Bomb]] and a [[Sunflower]].

It appears on a random spot of the lawn and explodes, dealing damage to zombies in an area of effect and stalling them for a short moment.

If there is a spawn plant in the explosion area it also gets healed slightly.
