Noctarine is a level 2 mutation obtained by splicing a [[Cherry Bomb]] and a [[Moonbean]]

It appears on a random spot of the lawn and explodes, dealing damage to zombies in an area of effect.

If the player has at least one [[Moonflower]], Noctarine also leaves behind a poison cloud that deals little damage to zombies in its area until it dissipates.
