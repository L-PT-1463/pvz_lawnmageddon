Grapheshot is a level 2 mutation obtained by fusing two [[Cherry Bomb]]s, or by applying [[Fertilizer]] to a Cherry Bomb.

It appears on a random spot of the lawn and explodes, dealing damage to zombies in an area of effect, and fires projectiles in random directions, which do a smaller explosion on impact.
