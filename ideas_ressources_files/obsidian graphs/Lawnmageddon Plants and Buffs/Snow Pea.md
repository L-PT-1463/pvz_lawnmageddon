Snow Pea is a level 2 mutation obtained by splicing a [[Peashooter]] and an [[Iceberg Lettuce]].

It attacks the nearest zombie to the player with a straight attack, firing a pea that chills zombies.

If there are more than one [[Peashooter.canvas]]-types, the second instead attacks the second closest zombie, and so on for the next.
