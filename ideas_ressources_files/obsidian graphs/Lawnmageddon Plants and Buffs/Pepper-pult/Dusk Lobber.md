Melon-pult is a level 2 mutation obtained by splicing a [[Pepper-pult]] and a [[Moonbean]].

It attacks the zombie with the highest max total hp in the lawn with a lobbed attack that deals splash damage.

If the player has at least one [[Moonflower]], Dusk Lobber lobs two additional buds that land near the main target.

If there are more than one [[Pepper-pult.canvas]]-types, the second instead attacks the second healthiest zombie, and so on for the next.
