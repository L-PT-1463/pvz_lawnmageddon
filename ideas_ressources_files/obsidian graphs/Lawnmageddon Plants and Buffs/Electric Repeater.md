Electric Repeater is a level 3 plant obtained by fusing a [[Peashooter]] and an [[Electric Peashooter]] or a [[Repeater]] and an [[E.M.Peach]].

It attacks the nearest zombie to the player with a straight attack, firing two peas that traverse the zombie to hit the other behind it.

If there are more than one [[Peashooter.canvas]]-types, the second instead attacks the second closest zombie, and so on for the next.
