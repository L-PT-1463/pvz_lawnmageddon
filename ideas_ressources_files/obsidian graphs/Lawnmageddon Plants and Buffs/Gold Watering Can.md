Gold Watering Can is a level 2 ability obtained by fusing two [[Watering Can]]s.

It temporarily boosts the attack speed of a random potted plant and its neighbours.

If a plant is already under the effects of a [[Watering Can.canvas]]-type ability's buff, this targets a different plant.
