Fume-shroom is a level 2 mutation obtained by fusing two [[Puff-shroom]]s, or by applying [[Fertilizer]] to a Puff-shroom.

It attacks the nearest zombie to the player with a straight attack, firing a short-ranged beam of fumes that can hit multiple zombies.

If there are more than one [[Puff-shroom.canvas]]-types, the second instead attacks the second closest zombie, and so on for the next.
