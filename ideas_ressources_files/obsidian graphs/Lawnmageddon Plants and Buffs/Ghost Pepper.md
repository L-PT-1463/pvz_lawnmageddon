Ghost Pepper is a level 3 plant obtained by fusing a [[Cherry Bomb]] and a [[Jalapeño]]

It appears on a random spot of the lawn, dealing damage to all zombies in a line stretching to a random direction, then explodes, dealing damage to all zombies in an area of effect.
