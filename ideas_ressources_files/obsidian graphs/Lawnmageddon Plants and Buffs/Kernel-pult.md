Kernel-pult is a base lobber plant.

It attacks the zombie with the highest max total hp in the lawn with a weak lobbed attack, but periodically lobs a stalling butter instead.

If there are more than one [[Kernel-pult.canvas]]-types, the second instead attacks the second healthiest zombie, and so on for the next.
