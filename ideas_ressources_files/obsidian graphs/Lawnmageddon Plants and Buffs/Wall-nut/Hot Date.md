Hot Date is a level 3 mutation obtained by splicing a [[Sweet Potato]] and a [[Cherry Bomb]], or by fusing an [[Explode-o-Nut]] and a [[Wall-nut]].

It appears on a random spot of the lawn and stays there until vanquished, exploding when that happens to deal damage to all zombies in a line stretching to a random direction. It attracts zombies in an area of effect towards it.
