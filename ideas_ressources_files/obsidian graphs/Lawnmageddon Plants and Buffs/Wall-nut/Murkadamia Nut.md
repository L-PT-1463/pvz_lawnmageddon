Murkadamia nut is a level 2 mutation obtained by splicing a [[Wall-nut]] and a [[Moonbean]].

It appears on a random spot of the lawn and stays there until vanquished.

If the player has at least one [[Moonflower]], Murkadamia Nut has increased hp and inflicts damage to zombie in a small area around itself.
