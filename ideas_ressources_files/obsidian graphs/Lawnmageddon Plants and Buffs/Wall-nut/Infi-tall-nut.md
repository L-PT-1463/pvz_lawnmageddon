Infi-tall-nut is a level 3 mutation obtained by fusing a [[Wall-nut]] and an [[Infi-nut]], or by applying [[Fertilizer]] to an Infi-nut.

A generator appears on a random spot of the lawn, and periodically generates a tall-nut projection.
