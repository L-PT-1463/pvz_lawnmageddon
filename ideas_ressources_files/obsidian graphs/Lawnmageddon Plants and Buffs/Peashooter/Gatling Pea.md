Gatling Pea is a level 4 mutation obtained by fusing a [[Peashooter]] and a [[Three Pod]], or by applying [[Fertilizer]] to a Three Pod.

It attacks the nearest zombie to the player with a straight attack, firing four peas.

If there are more than one [[Peashooter.canvas]]-types, the second instead attacks the second closest zombie, and so on for the next.
