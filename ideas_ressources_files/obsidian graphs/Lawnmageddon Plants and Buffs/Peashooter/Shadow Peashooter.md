Shadow Peashooter is a level 2 mutation obtained by splicing a [[Peashooter]] and a [[Moonbean]].

It attacks the nearest zombie to the player with a straight attack, firing a pea that slows down the hit zombie.

If the player has at least one [[Moonflower]], Shadow Peashooter instead shoots a laser that pierces through multiple zombies.

If there are more than one [[Peashooter.canvas]]-types, the second instead attacks the second closest zombie, and so on for the next.
