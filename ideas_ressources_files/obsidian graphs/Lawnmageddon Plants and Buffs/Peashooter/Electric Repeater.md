Electric Repeater is a level 3 mutation obtained by fusing a [[Peashooter]] and an [[Electric Peashooter]], or by splicing a [[Repeater]] and an [[E.M.Peach]], or by applying [[Fertilizer]] to an Electric Peashooter.

It attacks the nearest zombie to the player with a straight attack, firing two large peas that traverses the zombie to hit others behind it.

If there are more than one [[Peashooter.canvas]]-types, the second instead attacks the second closest zombie, and so on for the next.
