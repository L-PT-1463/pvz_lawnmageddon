Sling Pea is a level 4 mutation obtained by splicing [[Three Pod]] and [[Cabbage-pult]].

It always fires five peas, attacking the nearest five zombies to the player with a lobbed attack. If there are less than five zombies on screen, more than one pea can be targeted at each zombie.

If there are more than one [[Peashooter.canvas]]-types, the second instead attacks the second closest zombie, and so on for the next.
