Split-pea is a level 3 mutation obtained by fusing a [[Peashooter]] and a [[Repeater]].

It attacks the nearest zombie to the player with a straight attack, firing one pea forwards and one diagonally to each side.

If there are more than one [[Peashooter.canvas]]-types, the second instead attacks the second closest zombie, and so on for the next.
