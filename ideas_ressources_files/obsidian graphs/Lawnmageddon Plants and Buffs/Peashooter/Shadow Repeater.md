Shadow Repeater is a level 3 mutation obtained by fusing a [[Peashooter]] and an [[Shadow Peashooter]], or by splicing a [[Repeater]] and a [[Moonbean]], or by applying [[Fertilizer]] to an Shadow Peashooter.

It attacks the nearest zombie to the player with a straight attack, firing two pea that slows down the hit zombie.

If the player has at least one [[Moonflower]], Shadow Peashooter instead shoots two laser that pierces through multiple zombies.

If there are more than one [[Peashooter.canvas]]-types, the second instead attacks the second closest zombie, and so on for the next.
