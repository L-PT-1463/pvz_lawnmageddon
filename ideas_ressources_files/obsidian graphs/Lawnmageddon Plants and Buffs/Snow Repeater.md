Snow Repeater is a level 3 mutation obtained by fusing a [[Peashooter]] and a [[Snow Pea]], or by splicing a [[Repeater]] and an [[Iceberg Lettuce]], or by applying [[Fertilizer]] to a Snow Pea.

It attacks the nearest zombie to the player with a straight attack, firing two peas that chill zombies.

If there are more than one [[Peashooter.canvas]]-types, the second instead attacks the second closest zombie, and so on for the next.
