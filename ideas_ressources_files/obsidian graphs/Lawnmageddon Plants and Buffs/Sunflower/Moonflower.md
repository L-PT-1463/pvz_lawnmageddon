Moonflower is a level two mutation obtained by splicing a [[Sunflower]] and a [[Moonbean]].

It activates other shadow plants' powers.

If the player has at least one [[Moonflower]], Moonflower also generates a weak shield around the player that can take a few hits before having to be regenerated. It has a long cool down and does not heal.

If a plant is already under the effects of a [[Sunflower.canvas]]-type plant's buff, this targets a different plant.
