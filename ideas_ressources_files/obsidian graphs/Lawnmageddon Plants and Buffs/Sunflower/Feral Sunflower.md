Feral Sunflower is a level three mutation obtained by splicing a [[Primal Sunflower]] and a [[Chomper]].

It periodically generates rays of sun on other potted plants which temporarily increase their damage.

It attacks the nearest zombie to itself in range.

If a plant is already under the effects of a [[Sunflower.canvas]]-type plant's buff, this targets a different plant.
