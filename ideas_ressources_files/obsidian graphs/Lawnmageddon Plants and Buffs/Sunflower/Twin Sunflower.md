Twin Sunflower is a level two mutation obtained by fusing two [[Sunflower]]s, or by applying [[Fertilizer]] to a Sunflower.

It periodically generates rays of sun on two other potted plants which temporarily increase their attack speed.

If a plant is already under the effects of a [[Sunflower.canvas]]-type plant's buff, this targets a different plant.
